Problem:

The MyAnimeList(MAL) site allows users to keep track of the anime they've seen. However it does not give a lot of insight into your preferences and watch patterns, especially when the account owner has seen over a 100 different series. Mainly due to a lack of visualization tools on the site itself, which also results in not being able to compare to other users.

These visualizations can give insight on genre and studio preferences. For genre specifically in the context of total amount, seasonal and yearly specifics.
About MAL:

Within MAL you can categorize different series witin different lists to keep track of the anime series you've seen, put on-hold, dropped or planned to watch. Furthermore it gives extensive details from synopsis to staff members and overall popularity within the site itself.
The data is obtained by scraper, however it is currently not possible to call the scraper in the site or use other data besides the already scraped data.

My list of Anime on MAL
There are several interactable element present on the visualization page, which are:
  • The header with the submit, herein you can
    • Submit whoms data to use
    • Select which list to use
      • Completed: Completed Entries
      • On-Hold: Entries, which momentarily have been suspended
      • Dropped: Entries, which have been suspended. Most likely for an indefinite period of time
      • PTW:Entries which are Planned to Watch

  • The BarChart
    • The bars of the BarChart, which influence which genre is represented in the HeatMap
    • The options besides the BarChart

  • The HeatMap
    • The Slider, which influences the 10 year range of the HeatMap
    • The squares within the Heatmap, which influence the agenda beside the HeatMap

  • The LineChart
    • The lines of the LineChart, which influence which genre is represented in the HeatMap
    • The Checkboxes beside the LineChart
    • The CheckAll/unCheckAll buttons
